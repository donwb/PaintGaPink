"You've Been Pinked" is an online fundraiser by Breast Friends, Inc., a Georgia 501 (c)(3) non-profit orginization.

    • "Pink" Someone and Make Their Day!

    What does it mean to "Pink" someone? We invite you to "Pink" someone online by sending a You've Been Pinked Card to a friend, family member or someone touched by breast cancer.

    The online cards are $5 each, and the proceeds will go to lend emotional support, practical assistance, and information and resources for breast cancer patients, their family, and friends.
